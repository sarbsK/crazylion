Who I Am

HI! I am Sarbeswar, I was born and brought up in Assam. Presently, I am located in the beautiful city and Heart of North-East Guwahati, Assam. Currently I am trying to develop a AAA Game. Previously I was working as a game ARTIST at Malaysia's one of the reputed games company and before that art INSTRUCTOR at India's one of the reputed gaming institute and before that as a game ARTIST for a very old and reputed gaming company of India; worked on a couple of AAA game titles; for more than 8 years and also have a 3 year of experiences as a traditional portrait artist ..

My Work

My work has been in AAA titles to indie projects, and even Social games titles. I have a love for it all no matter whatever the platform it may be. My main passion is with texturing and my style sways heavily towards realism/semi realism/cartoony and I have a love for understanding light physics through photography and simulating it with my texturing. I am constantly learning and refining my process for sculpting, modeling and texturing..
